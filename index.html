About ROSALEX
Farm Enterprise

Nestled just outside Isinya town, our farm buzzes with life! Our fields flourish with Kenyan favorites - sukumawiki, spinach, kachumbari (khokho), and pungent onions. Beyond the vegetables, happy hens cluck contentedly, while sheep graze peacefully and pigs add a touch of charm. We don't stop there - our land boasts golden maize and vibrant french beans, making us a true one-stop shop for fresh produce from Isinya!
